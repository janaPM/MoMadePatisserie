### 2. How to Download Instagram Images

**Method 1: Browser Download**
1. Open the Instagram post in your browser
2. Right-click on the image
3. Select "Save image as..." or "Download image"
4. Save to the `src/assets/images/` folder with the correct filename

**Method 2: Online Download Tools**
- Use websites like "downloadgram" or "instadownloader"
- Paste the Instagram URL and download the image
- Rename to match the filenames above

### 3. Image Specifications

For best results, ensure images are:
- High resolution (at least 1200x800px)
- JPG format
- Well-lit and focused on the cakes
- Square or landscape orientation

### 4. Alternative: Use Direct Image URLs

If you prefer not to download, you can also update the component to use direct Instagram image URLs by modifying the image paths in `mo-made.component.ts`.

The website will automatically update once you add the images to the assets folder!
